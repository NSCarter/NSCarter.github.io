LEGO ROBOT

For my first university project, I had to work with a team to build and program a Lego robot that could fight in an arena against another robot. The aim was to push the opposing robot out of the arena.

The programming was a simple drag and drop. Because of my previous experience in programming, I took the lead with this aspect of the task. I had to program the sensors to detect certain things and react in a certain way. For example, we had a colour sensor that was used to detect the edge of the arena (which was a white strip). If it sensed the colour change, the robot would steer away from the edge and move back to the middle of the arena.

We also used Bluetooth to connect the robot to our phones so we could activate the robot without touching it.
